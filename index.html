Why PSA?

Its an honor to be leading this great student association that makes us all work together to promote our cultural uniqueness, diversity, cuisine and tourism amongst all the international community present in and around Aachen.

About Us

PSA Aachen is a non-profit organization representing Pakistani Students and community in Aachen. This association covers students at RWTH-Aachen University and FH-Aachen. Main objective of PSA is not only to help Pakistanis who are studying in Bachelors, Masters, PhD, or other programs at universities in Aachen but also to support Pakistanis with different issues related to studies, health, community, accommodation, student jobs. We also cater Pakistani students who come to Aachen from other cities for HiWi, Internship, or master thesis. Our mission is to develop good ties with the student organizations in Aachen and across Germany.

Every year, PSA-Aachen arranges Events and Recreational activities such as trips, parties, get-togethers for Pakistani students and community. All in all, our role is to develop a good image of Pakistan by creating awareness about its people, culture, landscape, traditions and food.
