Why PSA?

Its an honor to be leading this great student association that makes us all work together to promote our cultural uniqueness, diversity, cuisine and tourism amongst all the international community present in and around Koblenz.

About Us

PSA Koblenz is a student association and a non-profit organization that aims to facilitate Pakistani students and community in Koblenz. The primary objective of PSA is to help and guide Pakistani students during the course of their stay in Koblenz. Not only that, but also support them with a variety of issues concerning studies, health, accommodation, internships and jobs.

We also assist Pakistani students coming to Koblenz from other cities for internships or thesis. In a free and fast-track world where everyone is for himself, PSA aims to build a community that is bound with the principles of inclusivity and cohesion. Hence why, establishing friendly ties with student organizations in Koblenz and elsewhere in Germany remains our top priority. PSA Koblenz organizes several educational and recreational events each year which brings the community closer. These events include cultural shows, sports activities, excursion trips, friendly gatherings and parties. To sum it up, PSA Koblenz is not just a student organization but also a great propagator of the soft image of Pakistan that promotes it's culture, tradition, language and food.
